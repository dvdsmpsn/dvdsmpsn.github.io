Here's a sample of some projects that I've worked on in the last 2 or 3 years

Client work

  • AT&T – Upgrade from Atlassian Confluence version 3.5 to 5.x for "the world's largest Confluence instance" and much custom work to integrate Confluence with IBM Connections allowing the wiki to conform to AT&T's stringent governance requirements. Adapting AT&T's original Confluence customisations into plugins so that the subsequent upgrade path will be far more sustainable.

    Most of my interaction has been with David, whom I worked directly with during the developing of a plugin to encapsulate most of our default theme customizations.
